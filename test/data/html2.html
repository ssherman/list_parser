The 999 Greatest Works

This is our flagship list, the greatest works of literature ordered by date from ancient to recent times. It was first posted in 1999 and has been continually revised and updated ever since. It covers all creative literary forms, genres, cultures and eras.

How were these titles chosen? Why "999"? How has the list changed? See "Creating the Greatest Literature of All Time list".

This list is for major works. For the most part it excludes short works, which may however appear on the Greatest Stories, Greatest Short Poems or other lists.

For a shorter ranking of the most acclaimed works ever, see the Top 99 Works of Literature. And for an even more exhaustive list than this one, see the Really Long List of Great Literature.

Click linked titles and authors for details, critiques and comments.
